Overview

This post documents my media usage over a 24-hour period. Media includes all forms of information and entertainment consumption such as social media, streaming services, messaging apps, news platforms, music, video games, and search engines.

What I Discovered

I realized that I spend a significant amount of time playing video games on my days off. While this helps me relax and avoid burnout, I could likely complete my visual novel faster if I focused more consistently on it. School remains my top priority, but outside of that, I noticed that I have a lot of screen time. In the future, I may try to incorporate more outdoor activities that are not exercise-related into my routine.

Most Credible Media Sources

This transparency helps reduce my anxiety about misinformation and allows me to make more informed judgments about current events.
